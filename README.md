## ğŸ§  Insights Discovered  
From this analysis, several business insights were uncovered:

- **2004 was the strongest revenue year** during the reporting period.  
- **Classic Cars** is the highest performing product line by a large margin.  
- **USA is the top performing country**, followed by Spain and France.  
- Lower-performing categories such as Ships and Trains may require strategic review.  

These insights help companies understand product demand and market opportunities.
